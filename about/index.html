Xinnian Liang (梁新年)

Welcome to my homepage!

I am a final-year Ph.D. student studying at Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Zhoujun Li and Dr. Shuangzhi Wu. I obtained my B.S. degree at Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology. My research interest includes Large Language Models, Text Summarization, Information Extraction. I am currently mainly focusing on research related to large language model agents and their reasoning and planning capabilities.

In my spare time, I have diverse interests and enjoy activities such as football, cycling, 王者荣耀, coffee, whiskey, cocktails, and more. I welcome anyone with common interests to engage in discussions and exchange thoughts. If you have any questions and would like to communicate with me, you can reach me through the email address xnliang19@163.com.
